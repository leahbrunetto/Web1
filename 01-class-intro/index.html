Leah Brunetto

MIT Class of 2012, Department of Architecture

This semester, I would like to launch an exciting website to function as an art portfolio. I currently display and write about my work on a Wordpress blog, which is functional and easy to work with, but I would like to have the ability to design my own site and to have my own domain name. I also hope that learning web design will help me to find a job after I graduate this spring. While I have enjoyed participating in projects in academia and at museums, I would very much like to have the ability to do freelance work or work for a company. Overall, I hope that the skills I gain this semester will give me the power to create whatever I want, increase my understanding of how the Web works, and make me a more desirable candidate in the job market.
