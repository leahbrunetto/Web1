Shore Cycle

Fall 2011

As inspired by a talk by Robert Wilson, my performance will exist in a “megastructure” of four acts: “Grey Seal”, “Great White Shark”, “Tourist”, and “Fisherwoman”. These are all characters “at the edge of the sea”, which are linked to the Cape Cod tourism industry and the related predator-prey relationships. I will perform as each of these characters—using costume, hand made and found props, movement, and projected animations. Both the animations (to be drawn by hand but manipulated mostly programmatically in Processing) and props will act as transitions, as well as accompany these acts, as inspired by the surrealist, stream of consciousness style in Monty Python’s Flying Circus. One character transformation will occur behind the white board, while the other two will occur in front of the audience. For audio, I will combine ambient electronic music with found and original sound effects; this audio will be embedded in the movie file of the animations. .
